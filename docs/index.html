About our server

We are a Filipino Christian Discord community focused on Gospel-based nation-building aiming to proclaim the goodness of God and purpose of His holiness in our lives. Our server aims to teach Biblical values regardless of any denomination involved and providing joy of fellowship.

The server was founded on 19th of February, 2021 by Stephen L., with select invitees of a random Discord server from different archipelagos and overseas to kickstart the development of a Filipino server with the focus of edifying each other in the faith. Not entirely a popular server, but our scope is to reach more kababayans with different varieties including talents or perhaps apologetics, especially unbelievers who want to seek God and His righteousness.

Statement of Faith

Philippines Is For Christ is a Non-Denominational Christian Server seeking to have fellowship as one body in faith. We are not affiliated with any organization in and out of the country.

A. The Bible, the Word of God, its divine, verbal, plenary inspiration and its inerrancy and infallibility as originally written; and its supreme and final authority in faith and life (2 Tim.3:16; 2 Pet.1:20-21);

B. One God eternally existing in three distinct Persons, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (Deut. 6:4, Matt. 28:19, 2 Cor. 13:14);

C. The Lord Jesus Christ:
1. His essential, absolute and eternal Deity (Phil. 2:6; John 1:1,14: Heb. 1:8)
2. His true and sinless humanity (1Pet. 2:22; 1 John 3:5)
3. His Virgin Birth (Isa. 7:14; Matt. 1:20)
4. His substitutionary, propitiatory death (Matt. 20:28; Mark 10:45, 1Tim 2:16)
5. His bodily resurrection (Acts 1:11)
6. His ascension to the right hand of the Father (Mark 16:19; Acts 1:20)
7. His coming again with the power and great glory (Titus 2:13; Rev. 10:11-16)

D. The Holy Spirit who shows the redeeming purpose of God [in] the world by convicting the world of sin, of righteousness and judgment and by regenerating, uniting to Christ, indwelling, sanctifying, illuminating and empowering for service all who place complete faith in the Lord Jesus Christ (Heb. 9:14, John 14:26, 1 Cor. 2:10-11, 1 Cor. 12:11);

E. The total depravity of Man because of the Fall (Gen. 3:10-24);

F. Salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ apart from works (Eph. 2:8);

G. The everlasting bliss of the saved and the eternal suffering of the lost (Dan. 12:2; John 5:24; Rev. 20:14; Luke 16:24-26);

H. The real spiritual unity in Christ of all redeemed by His precious Blood (1 Cor. 12:13; Eph. 1:4-6; 4:11-15, 5:25,26); and

I. The necessity of maintaining, according to the Word of God, the purity of the Church in doctrine and life (1 Cor. 6:19,20; 1 Thess. 4.3).

*All and these are taken from the Philippine Council of Evangelical Churches' page.
